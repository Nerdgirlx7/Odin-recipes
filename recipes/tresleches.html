Tres Leches

Infamous Tres Leches Cake

tres leches Cake (meaning 3 milks) is a traditional staple dessert in mexican households. The Tres Leches cake consist of a light sponge cake soaked in a mixture of sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and heavy cream, often topped with whipped cream and dessert garnish like strawberries, peaches, almond shavings and other wonderful treats like cookies, strawberry syrup and chocolate syrup drizzles.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • 5 Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Evaporated Milk 12 oz
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk 14 oz
  • Heavy Cream 1 1/2 cups
  • Whipped cream
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Fruit
  • Almond Shavings
  • Chocolate or strawberry syrup

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. butter and flour a 9-inch cake pan.

  2. In a bowl place the yolks. Slowly add sugar while beating on high. Mix until the yolks are nice and fluffy. Mix in the Vanilla.

  3. Sift together the flour, salt and baking powder.

  4. In another bowl beat the whites until they form soft peaks.

  5. Slowly fold Whites into the yolk mixture with spatula.

  6. Next, slowly sift the flour mixture into the white and yolk mixture and fold ingredients carefully. Try not to deflate the air whipped into the eggs.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Place in center rack, and bake for 35-40 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

  8. In a larger pitcher whisk together evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and whole milk.

  9. cake has cooled poke holes with a fork.

  10. Pour the milk mixture over the cake and allow it soak for an hour in the refrigerator before adding the whipped topping.

  11. whipping cream with powdered sugar until thick and forms peaks.

  12. Spread the whipped cream over the top of the cake. refrigerate for at least an hour to allow the milk to soak in.

  13. with a sprinkle of cinnamon, fresh fruit, and other garnishes.

  14. Time to enjoy!
